ADL's Controlled Vocabulary

A controlled xAPI vocabulary is a predefined list of verbs and activities created and maintainted by an organization or community of interest. You may want to determine whether your organization will define a controlled vocabulary or if you will reference existing ones.

Controlled vocabularies ensure that the intended meaning of your statements are clearly understood and consistently conform to agreed-upon standards. This helps you avoid ambiguity in the interpretation of xAPI results data, because the semantic application and meaning of the terminology are commonly understood by everyone in your organization. The site provides a list of ADL's controlled vocabulary of xAPI verbs and activities to serve as a starting point. If you would like to submit a new activity or verb for consideration as part of the ADL vocabulary, please contact us.

Note: Submissions should be relevant to the Distributed Learning (DL) domain.
